What is Bench Press Ben?

Bench Press Ben is a game about a man named Ben who is charged with the responsibility to throw a heavy weight at nuclear chickens before they hit the ground. He also must recapture that same weight because dropping it would cramp Ben's style. This game is a work in progress. It's being developed for Android and iphone but I have ported it over to HTML5 so that the alpha version is playable in your browser! Bugs are to be expected given its current alpha state.

Who is Ben?

Ben is just your average joe with huge muscles. You control Ben in Bench Press Ben and work to defend the surface from the tyranny of the nuclear chickens. You use the arrows keys to move Ben and press 'Z' to throw the weight at the chickens raining from above or you can use the onscreen-buttons for the same purpose.

What's a Cyclobat?

Cyclobat has only one eye and a big red sore on its head. Like nuclear chickens, these critters are dangerous. In order to knock one out, the weight ben throws will have to land on Cyclobat's sore on its head. He may only have one eye but he cleans up well on prom night.

PreWorkout?!?

Want to get Ben even more pumped!?! Then snatch a preWorkout powerup. These powerups periodically float down. Be careful NOT to break one of these powerups with Ben's weight. Let it float down to Ben.
